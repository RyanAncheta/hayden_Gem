Butter Chicken Roti


The restaurant offers Indian flatbread called Roti, filled with traditional Indian curries, served as wraps or separately. The Saag Paneer Roti, with smooth saag and large chunks of paneer, is highly recommended. The roti is super thin, and the portion size is generous. The spice level is hot, making it a great choice for spice lovers. Overall, a delicious and satisfying dining experience with a variety of flavorful options to try.

Fat Pasha


Fat Pasha is a funky eatery in Toronto with a country-chic decor, serving delicious Middle Eastern and European Jewish dishes. Their standout item is the best lamb chops in town, but the menu offers plenty of other flavorful options, including cocktails. The roasted cauliflower and refreshing fattoush are must-tries. The relaxed vibe and courteous staff make it a great spot for a date or hanging out with friends. The restaurant also offers a tempting brunch menu. Overall, it's a highly recommended dining experience worth trying!

Hong Fatt B-B-Q


This Chinese BBQ place in Chinatown is a go-to spot, offering delicious food, generous portions, and quick service at affordable prices. The restaurant has simple decorations and is run by a husband and wife, with family helping in the kitchen for soup and noodle dishes. Cash is the only accepted payment method. The BBQ pork is a standout dish, but there are other tasty options available on the hot table. The soup is also highly recommended, especially during winter. Overall, it's a great place to enjoy flavorful Chinese BBQ and comforting soups.

Dainties Macaron


This charming little shop offers a delightful variety of macarons with Asian-inspired flavors, filled with buttercream. The real standout is their macaron ice cream sandwich, which completely impressed me. Unlike others I've tried in the past, this one had perfectly chewy macarons and delicious flavors. The jasmine tea macaron was skillfully executed but could have been more flavorful. Overall, the shop boasts excellent presentation and high-quality macaron shells, making it a place I'd definitely return to try more in the future.

Delysees is a luxury bakery in downtown Toronto known for serving incredible desserts with above & beyond service. They specialize in top-of-the-line French pastries, Macarons, Cakes, Eclairs, and classic French baked goods. They also offer a variety of modern gluten-free and vegan desserts. Dessert fox


Dessert Fox Toronto is a delightful place that offers Croffles, a combination of croissants and waffles. The Spicy Salmon Sashimi Croffle impresses with marinated salmon, fresh spring mix, and a kick of Korean chili sauce. For a sweet treat, the Blueberry Cream Croffle with blueberries and cream cheese is a luxurious delight. Don't miss the refreshing Matcha Strawberry Latte, a perfect ending to your croffle experience. A must-visit spot for a unique and enjoyable dining adventure!

Harbourfront Canoe & Kayak Centre


The Harbourfront Canoe & Kayak Centre in Toronto is a must-go paddling destination offering kayak, canoe, and stand-up paddleboard rentals and instruction. With scenic Toronto Islands just a short paddle away, it provides the perfect setting for relaxation and skill development. The center fosters a strong social community, leading to lasting friendships and even some marriages. They offer a range of activities, including guided tandem kayak tours, team-building programs, and private big canoe excursions for groups. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced paddler, this center is a fantastic place to explore the waters and enjoy the beauty of Toronto's waterfront.

Kensington Market


Kensington Market is a bohemian neighborhood in Toronto, popular among artists and tourists. It boasts indie shops, vintage boutiques, and arts spaces. The area also offers a wide array of specialty grocers, bakeries, and cheese shops. Trendy bars, cafes, and international restaurants attract hipsters, while Victorian houses line the streets for students and families. A vibrant and diverse destination with a unique atmosphere.

Wet'n'Wild Toronto


Your ultimate water park oasis in the heart of Brampton, Ontario, Canada! A 100-acre complex filled with an array of exhilarating attractions. Get ready for an action-packed day as you take on numerous water slides, make a splash in the wave pool, relax in the lazy river, and feel the adrenaline rush on our four-lane zip-line. Families and little adventurers will have a blast at our two kid's splash areas. Come and experience non-stop fun and adventure at Wet'n'Wild Toronto - the perfect destination to beat the heat and make unforgettable memories!

Illumi Missisauga


Illumi is a captivating nocturnal outdoor journey, featuring thousands of monumental light sculptures. With over 20 million brilliant lights in 13 magical universes across a 600,000 square feet site, it's an enchanting experience visible from kilometers away. The centerpiece is the majestic 47-meter-high Tree of Lights, crafted from 28,000 interactive luminous spheres, radiating magical grace and wonder.
