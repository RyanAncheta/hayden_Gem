Welcome to Downtown Toronto Hidden Restaurants!

Discover the true essence of Downtown Toronto's culinary landscape as we unveil its hidden treasures and underrated gems.

Our mission is to offer a unique and refreshing perspective on the dining scene, ensuring unforgettable experiences and moments of relaxation during the holiday season.
Embrace the opportunity to celebrate the city's culinary delights, delving into a world beyond the bustling metropolis stereotype.

So, join us on this gastronomic journey, and let's unravel the lesser-known culinary tapestry of Downtown Toronto, bringing an array of delectable surprises to your palate.

As you wander through charming streets and modern avenues, diverse cultural influences and innovative culinary craftsmanship fill the air with enticing aromas. From tucked-away bistros with their tantalizing fusion creations to family-owned bakeries carrying on generational pastry recipes, Toronto's food scene reveals itself in every bite. Venture beyond the usual options to find a mosaic of flavors reflecting the city's history and cosmopolitan character. About the Authors:

Jercy - (tiong.axel@gmail.com)
Jercy is a Graduate student studying in the Information Technology Solutions graduate program at Humber International Graduate School.

He has been living in Great Toronto Area since September 2018. He recently graduated from the University of Toronto with the Honour Bachelor of Science degree (Applied Statistics).

After living for 5 years in the GTA, I have heard a lot of people, especially Taiwanese, complaining how Downtown Toronto is just another boring big city like how we feel about Taipei, Taiwan. Although Downtwon Toronto has a lot of similarities like Taipei, there are still unique parts of Downtown that I found out recently where the spots could overturn the established impression of people who have been or are going to live here. That is what motivates us to create this website as our project for this introduction course. We hope to use what we learned in this course to display a brand new and original website that could help everyone, whether they are the newcomers or permanant residants, to discover the "hidden gems" of Downtown Toronto.
If possible, we hope the list in the website could be continuously updated with the reviews of visitors of our websites, so Downtown Toronto could always be interesting for every visitor. Hopefully, my experiences in Downtown Toronto could provide some options onto the list too.
